Contacting the Library

Special Collections Research Accounts are used in the Department of Rare Books and Special Collections in the Firestone Library, the Seeley G. Mudd Library, the Marquand Library of Art and Archaeology, and the East Asian Library. Questions should be directed to the library that you plan to visit:

  • Rare Books and Special Collections, Firestone Library
    rbsc@princeton.edu or (609) 258-3184
    http://www.princeton.edu/~rbsc/index.shtml

  • Seeley G. Mudd Manuscript Library
    mudd@princeton.edu or (609) 258-6345
    http://www.princeton.edu/mudd/

  • Marquand Library of Art and Archaeology
    marquand@princeton.edu or (609) 258-3783
    http://marquand.princeton.edu/rare-books

  • East Asian Library and the Gest Collection
    mheijdra@princeton.edu or gest@princeton.edu or (609) 258-5336
    http://eastasianlib.princeton.edu/usingrb.htm
